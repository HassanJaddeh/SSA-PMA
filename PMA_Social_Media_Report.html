Key insight — quality over quantity

LinkedIn is the clearest evidence in this dataset of a quality-over-quantity effect. Posting 33.8 times/month in 2023 produced an average of 4.5 engagements. Posting 16.4 times/month in 2025 produced 25.3 — 5.6× higher output from roughly half the volume. Optimal LinkedIn cadence for PMA appears to be 12–20 posts per month focused on high-quality institutional content.

The viral content template

Every high-performing post shares the same five characteristics: (1) Consumer protection or financial safety topic with direct, immediate personal relevance. (2) A single, actionable message the audience can apply within 24 hours. (3) Video format, under 60 seconds. (4) Published across Facebook, Instagram, and LinkedIn simultaneously. (5) Arabic-language, accessible tone — not institutional or regulatory language.

Methodology note

Engagement figures are derived from public post scrapes (Facebook.csv, Instagram.csv). Comment sentiment uses a rule-based Arabic classifier combining a curated lexicon of 80+ positive/negative terms, emoji signal detection, and VADER for Latin-script portions. 609 of 1,341 Facebook posts (45%) and 179 of 1,775 Instagram posts (10%) have matched scraped comments. Posts without matched comments are included in engagement totals but excluded from sentiment analysis. Native platform exports (reactions breakdown, reach, impressions) are available separately and will be integrated in the full report.
